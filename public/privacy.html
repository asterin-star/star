Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 21, 2025

1. Overview

Star Oracle ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our Mini App within World App.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect or store any personal identification information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.

When you use our services, we may process:

  • Wallet Address: To verify payments for premium features.
  • Transaction IDs: To confirm successful payments on the blockchain.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous statistics about card selections to improve the Oracle's responses.

3. Use of Information

We use the limited data we collect solely to:

  • Facilitate transactions via World MiniKit.
  • Provide the requested Tarot reading services.
  • Verify proof of payment.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app operates within the World App ecosystem. Payments are processed through the World Chain blockchain using MiniKit. Please refer to Worldcoin's privacy policy for information on how they handle your data.

We use Google Vertex AI to generate reading interpretations. No personal user data is shared with Google; only the archetypal card data is processed.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use local storage on your device to save your session preference (e.g., selected language). We do not use third-party tracking cookies.
